CodeZ-TechCon 2025

Bridging Full Stack & AI Innovation
July 25–27, 2025 | Trivandrum Techno Park, India

The Most Ultimate 3-Day Tech Event by CodeZ Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
Unlock the Future of Full Stack Web Development and Artificial Intelligence from July 25–27, 2025 at Trivandrum Tech Park.

Featured Speakers

Mr. Aditya Menon

Full Stack Architect
Google, India

Aditya will talk about how websites and apps are built using frontend and backend technologies. He’ll share how to become a complete full stack developer step by step.

Dr. Rajeev Krishnan

Chief AI Scientist

Dr. Rajeev will explain how full stack web apps can be powered with AI features like chatbots, predictions, and smart automation — making apps more intelligent and user-friendly.

Conference Schedule

Day 1 – Foundations of Full Stack Excellence

Start your journey with the foundation of modern web development. This day focuses on how to build complete web applications using frontend and backend technologies. Topics include HTML, CSS, JavaScript frameworks like React, and backend tools like Node.js and Spring Boot. You'll also explore how databases, APIs, and deployment tools come together to create real-world applications.

Mr. Aditya Menon will guide participants through best practices for building responsive UIs, secure backend systems, and how to connect them smoothly. Expect hands-on coding sessions, interactive Q&A, and a live demo of a full stack project.

By the end of Day 1, you'll have a clear understanding of how to become a full stack developer and build your own professional web apps from scratch.

Day 2 – Full Stack Meets AI

Day 2 is all about combining what you learned on Day 1 with the power of Artificial Intelligence. You’ll discover how to add intelligent features like chatbots, recommendation systems, and smart automation to your websites and apps. Sessions will show how APIs like OpenAI, Google AI, and machine learning models can be integrated into full stack projects.

Led by Dr. Rajeev Krishnan, the sessions will simplify AI concepts and help you use tools like Python, Flask, and REST APIs to bring real AI functions into your web applications. No advanced math or theory — just real, practical examples for developers.

Hands-on labs will walk you through building AI-enabled apps step by step. This day helps bridge the gap between coding and intelligence, making your apps smarter and more useful.

Day 3 – Understanding AI & Future Trends

The final day takes a step back to explore what Artificial Intelligence really is, how it works behind the scenes, and how it is shaping the future. Topics will include natural language processing, computer vision, AI in everyday apps, and ethical concerns around AI usage.

Ms. Riya will explain AI in simple words, helping beginners understand concepts like training, learning, and prediction. She’ll also discuss real-world use cases in education, healthcare, and social apps.

In addition, Day 3 offers career-building advice, resume reviews, and expert talks on how to become a Full Stack AI Engineer. The day ends with a powerful keynote that discusses the future of technology, sustainability, and innovation in India and beyond.
